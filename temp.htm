Accredited Home Lenders Holding Co. was a San Diego-based mortgage banking company established in 1990/1999 that specialized in subprime mortgage loans, providing financing to borrowers who did not qualify for traditional loans. Following the 2007 subprime mortgage crisis, the company faced a severe liquidity crisis, was acquired by Lone Star Funds, and subsequently filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy in May 2009.
