Genesis and Artemis companies

When I searched for patents owned by Genesis Medical Technologies, I found that there was another company called Genesis Medical Technologies in California, even though I don't know if it's connected to the company of the Callender family in Colorado. [https://patents.google.com/?q=%28%22GENESIS+MEDICAL+TECHNOLOGIES+Inc%22%29&oq=%22GENESIS+MEDICAL+TECHNOLOGIES+Inc%22] The company in California owned patents related to medical devices. In 2001 several patents of the company had the name of the owner changed to Artemis Medical. Richard Fulton co-founded Artemis Medical in 2000, but he also co-founded a company called Genesis Medical Interventional in 2003, which developed a device for removing blood clots. [https://rocketreach.co/richard-fulton-email_5677054]

BCCI had two addresses listed in the Bahamas, which were in the building of Ansbacher, and in the Genesis Building that was built for a company called Genesis. One of the companies that now has an office in the Genesis Building is a finance company called Artemis Consultancy Ltd., whose address was listed as "3rd Floor, Genesis Building, Genesis Close". [https://artemisconsultancy.com] However I don't know if the company even occupies the entire third floor of building, because a company called Sinclair Group also has its address listed as the third floor of the Genesis Building. [https://www.sinclairs.com.ky/contact-cfvgi] The building also contains the offices of several other companies, including Kensington Management Group, McGrath Tonner, C.S Gill Attorneys, and Minerva Group. [https://mcgrathtonner.com/contact, https://mcgrathtonner.com/contact, https://www.ofreg.ky/register-of-licensees/foster-cayman-ltd-1, https://minerva.ky/contact-us/]

SuperClubs and John Issa

In the Paradise Papers, Ansbacher Bahamas is the owner of Red Car Company, which is a shareholder of Overseas Limited, which is one of the few shareholders of Super Clubs. [https://offshoreleaks.icij.org/nodes/80030159, https://offshoreleaks.icij.org/nodes/82022060] John Issa and Minna Issa are listed as directors of SuperClubs: [https://offshoreleaks.icij.org/nodes/82022060]

SuperClubs is a holiday resort company of John Issa: "The first all-inclusive packages in the Caribbean were introduced in Jamaica, based on the concept developed by Club Med and the cruise industry. The scheme offered tourists a combination of prized ingredients: including security, entertainment and, above all, vacations with no complications or surprises, since everything was paid for. In 1976, John Issa - currently the chairman of SuperClubs - opened the Negril Beach Village Resort, known today as Hedonism II. The young-singles image of the all-inclusive concept continued until 1978, when a complex for couples opened in Ocho Rios. In 1987, Issa founded the first all-inclusive family hotel." [https://digitallibrary.un.org/record/676640/files/S0900391_en.pdf] Hedonism II describes themselves as "the world's most iconic all-inclusive, clothing-optional adult resort". [https://www.instagram.com/hedonismjamaica/]

John Issa sold Hedonism II in 2012 to Harry Lange, who was previously the manager of the Fidelity Magellan fund, which is perhaps the best-known actively managed fund in the world. [https://www.forbes.com/sites/amyfeldman/2016/02/05/second-careers-ex-fidelity-magellan-fund-manager-harry-lange-is-the-money-man-at-hedonism-ii-resort/]

Hedonism II even has their own charitable foundation called the Hedonism II Foundation, which supports Jamaican schools: [https://linktr.ee/hedonismjamaica]
